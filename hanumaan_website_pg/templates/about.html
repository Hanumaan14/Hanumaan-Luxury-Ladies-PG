Our Story

A Safe Haven for Every Woman in Chennai

Hanumaan Luxury Ladies PG was founded with one goal: to give every lady a safe, comfortable, and affordable place to call home when living away from family in Chennai.

We are located at No:10, Gangai Street, Bharathi Nagar, Tharamani, Chennai – 600113 — strategically placed near major IT hubs like Tidel Park, Ascendas IT Park, and SIPCOT IT Park, making us the top choice for working women and students.

We treat every resident like family. From the moment you walk in, you'll feel the warmth, security, and comfort of home.
